WHAT DO WE DO WITH YOUR INFORMATION?

When you signup or book a service, as part of the booking process, we collect the personal information you give us such as your name, contact no., address and email ID.

When you browse our website, we also automatically receive your computer’s internet protocol (IP) address, which helps us knowyour browser and operating system.

We share the provided details with the service provider in order to fulfil your requirement. We do not share your information with all the service providers, but only with the service provider selected by you.In case the service provider is busy, we will share your details with an alternate service provider and also notify you on this.

CONSENT

After opting in, if you wish to withdraw your consent for us to contact you and use/disclosure of your information you can contact us at info@servicetree.in

PAYMENT

We use PayTM, Instamojo and Razorpay for processing payments. We do not store your card data on their servers. The data is encrypted through the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS),whileprocessing payment. Your card data is used only until your purchase transaction is completed.

Our payment gateway adheres to the standards set by PCI-DSS, managed by the PCI Security Standards Council, which collaborates with brands like Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover.

PCI-DSS requirements help ensure the secure handling of credit card information by our store and its service providers.
